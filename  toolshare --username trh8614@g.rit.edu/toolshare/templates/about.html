About toolshare!




The toolshare website allows for local communities to easily share their tools among one other in an organized manor. You can check out a tool from a neighbour or submit your own tool for others to borrow! Each member has their own profile to keep track of their activity as well as monitor their tools activity. Getting started is easy! Just hit the register button up top and begin your sharing!
